Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GAWE 2025, including its theme, objectives, and how you can contribute.

What is GAWE?

The Global Action Week for Education (GAWE) is a significant annual advocacy campaign dedicated to championing the right to quality education for all. GAWE is coordinated by the Global Campaign for Education (GCE), which sets the annual theme and provides campaign resources to ensure cohesive advocacy efforts worldwide. The Global Action Week for Education is embraced by National coalitions like the Tanzania Education Network/ Mtandao wa Elimu Tanzania (TEN/MET), Zambia National Education Coalition (ZANEC) among many. These efforts ensure that African priorities are reflected in the broader GAWE agenda. Countries commemorate GAWE through localized campaigns led by education coalitions and civil society organizations.

What is the theme for GAWE 2025?

The theme for GAWE 2025 is Education for Skills Development. This theme highlights the importance of equipping youth with essential skills for personal growth, creativity, and sustainable development. GAWE 2025 aims to raise awareness, advocate for skill-based education policies, and mobilize resources to enhance learning opportunities for all.

Who organizes GAWE 2025?

GAWE 2025 is coordinated globally by the Global Campaign for Education (GCE) and locally organized in Tanzania by the Tanzania Education Network / Mtandao wa Elimu Tanzania (TEN/MET) in collaboration with its member organizations.

What are the key goals of GAWE 2025?

The main goals of GAWE 2025 include:

  • Advocating for inclusive and equitable education that promotes skills development.
  • Raising awareness about the importance of equipping youth with essential skills for innovation and sustainable growth.
  • Engaging governments and stakeholders to strengthen policies that support skill-based education.
  • Empowering communities to champion education systems that foster creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Where can I find resources and updates after GAWE 2025?

Resources, reports, and updates from GAWE 2025 will be available on our website tenmet.or.tz and shared through our official social media channels. Participants and stakeholders will also receive email newsletters featuring key highlights and follow-up actions.

For sponsorship opportunities, please contact us at info@tenmet.or.tz or coordinator@tenmet.or.tz.
